# DDLOCK
**TAGLINE:** Lock your digital distractions, unlock your focus
# What is DDLOCK?
DDLOCK is a productivity app that helps users stay focused for a set amount of time. it adopts the concept of "DND" mode, ALARMY and Alarm clock creating a single platform that reduces digital distraction and enforce discipline.
# Why It Matters
In the world of today, people gets distracted easily by their mobile phone just by social media notification or random app alert which often leads to procastination or totally abandoning their task. Many try to rely on self discpline but still end up failing. DDLOCK will give user the structure needed to stay productive by helping users control their screen time and focus on meaningful tasks.
# Who it is for
DDLOCK is for: 
Students who easily gets distracted by the mobile phone and lose concentration due to this distractions while studying.
Professionals who struggle to stay focused at work because they are too engrossed in one activty or the other on their mobile phone.
Anyone in general trying to reduce their phone usage and increase their productivity.
# Use cases Examples
1. Tope, a student gets distracted by tiktok while studying.
DDLOCK SOLUTION: help her to lock all distracting app away until her study ends.
2. Aaron, after locking his app features away loses focuses one hour into his work because of a notification 
DDLOCK SOLUTION: Gives Aaron a brain tasking challenge that must be answered correctly under a set time in order to unlock his phone, discouraging his impulsive behaviour.
# Key features
1. Feature Lock Mode - Choose how long you want your phone’s features locked.  
2. Session Alarm - Alarm rings once your focus session is complete.  
3. Extended Lock Mode - Option to extend focus time after alarm rings.  
Challenge Unlock - Try to unlock early? Answer a logic or math question first.  
4. Emergency Unlock – Allows temporary access (5 minutes every 30 minutes) for urgent needs.  
# Technologies
1. Frontend: Flutter (for building mobile app interface that works on both Android and iOS)
2. Backend: Firebase (for storing user data, authentication and session timing)
3. Database: Cloud Firestore (for saving user lock preferences and session history)
4. Notifications: Firebase cloud messaging (for sending reminders and alarms)
5. Device APIs: Android/iOS system APIs (to manage lock features and notifications)
6. Version Control: Git & GitHub (for collaboration and code tracking)
7. Design: Figma or Canva (for app UI/UX mockups)
